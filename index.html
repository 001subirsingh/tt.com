ABOUT US
Techtitude tribe was founded by a group of students with a vision of providing an easier way to help others transform their amazing ideas into the real world solutions, so that they may not feel that setting up a startup is a rocket-science. Its motive is also to promote make-in-India moment. It is currently working and helping implement 6 great innovative ideas into the real world so that young entrepreneurs from around the nation may get help. The startups which are being implemented under this organization are ‘Adharshila’, ‘Shilpart’, ‘Handycart’, ‘Drugabuse’, ‘Attachaki’, etc. and more and more startups are being added day-by-day to our organization.
